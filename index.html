Code Quiz Rules

You will have only 60 seconds to complete the quizz.
You will get 5 points for each correct answer.
You will get 5 seconds penalty for each wrong answer.
You can't change your answer. Clicking on an answer will record it and take you to the next question.
You can't exit from the Quiz before the time is over or you answer all questions.
You will see your score at the end of the quiz and will have the option to save it.
